The Flappy 3D, written in Python, uses external engines but purely based on its built-in module TKinter.

Final Demo Video

This is a fun game and a remastered version of the popular game Flappy Bird. The game includes four playing mode: 2-Dimension, 3-Dimension, 3-Dimension Dual Player, and Reversed (smash birds with pipes). When I went to the critique session, I found my TA extremely obsessed with this game and trying to challenge my historical record.

This is my first project after I decided to switch out of the School of Architecture. But I still identified myself an artist, and thus I spent an enormous amount of energy tweaking around pixels. Unlike my many other friends, I really enjoyed it -- and this marked the very beginning of my User Interface Designer career.
